CommandTM

"I Shall Freeze You!"
- I Didn't Say That Vexen Did, Source

CommandTM (Miku) is the main protagonist of the yuri manga Citrus as well as its 2018 anime adaptation. She is voiced by Saki Fujita in the Japanese version of the anime, and by Saki Fujita in the English version of the anime. Along with that, CommandTM is also an author of the fan fiction.
