Do You Think You're Able To Kill Coach In Heavy Rain? is the 16th chapter of the fan fiction. The first act focuses on Trike Mike's introduction and ends with Jeff Jeffson XXX attacking The Fellowship of The Toppings and declaring his intention to kill Coach. The second act unveils the trap that Jigsaw prepared for Richard Nixon and SpongeBob, and the third act reveals Coach accidentally stole Jeff's gun
