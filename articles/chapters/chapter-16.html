Do You Think You're Able To Kill Coach In Heavy Rain? (Chapter 16)

"Hello Richard. I want to play a game. Forgive the restraints, I’m sure you’re not used to being handcuffed, thanks to your Vice President Crook."
- Jiggy's monologue, The Second Part of Chapter 16

Do You Think You're Able To Kill Coach In Heavy Rain? is the 16th chapter of the fan fiction. The first part focuses on Trike Mike's introduction and ends with Jeff Jeffson XXX attacking The Fellowship of the Toppings and declaring his intention to kill Coach. The second part unveils the trap that Jigsaw prepared for Richard Nixon and SpongeBob. The third part reveals that Coach accidentally stole Jeff's gun.

Plot Summary

Part 1

Following Trike Mike's arrival, Red Herring questions who he is. As Trike begins to make his way across the parking lot, Red Herring asks the rest of The Fellowship if that's Jersey Mike, and Luigi explains that Trike is the son of Jersey Mike and a high-ranking official, before Kenny cuts him off to clarify with clear disdain that Trike's role as the resistance's Secretary of Transportation is solely the result of nepotism, a sentiment which Coach agrees with. It is then revealed that Trike arrived while Luigi was still talking and was clearly impacted by what everyone said. After everyone gets on Trike's motorcycle, Luigi tries to diffuse the situation by saying that Kenny's harsh words were the result of stress. Kenny doubles down and goes off on Trike Mike, revealing that part of his bitterness towards him is because the resistance passed over him in favor of Trike for the role of Secretary of Transportation. At the end of Kenny's rant, Trike's bike is unexpectedly shot as Jeff Jeffson XXX arrives on horseback, intending to kill Coach.

Part 2

Richard Nixon wakes up handcuffed in the bathroom of Harry Reid International Airport. The bathroom was closed for rennovations, and there is a large gap in the ceiling, with a toolbag, ladder, and pane of glass on the floor beneath it. Also present is SpongeBob, who is dehydrated and chained to a cleaned urinal. After Nixon is done looking around, Jigsaw uses the airport intercom to explain Nixon's trap, which involves him "seal(ing) the watergate" using the tools at his disposal, with SpongeBob serving as his opponent, as he would presumably want the room to flood so that he can be rehydrated.

Part 3

We return to the streets of Vegas, where Jeff says Coach stole his iconic Magazine Fed Revolver, continuing to shoot at Trike's bike. While Jeff and Trike try to talk things out, everyone else looks to Coach for an explanation. Coach explains that he stole the gun by accident, as Jeff had left his gun in the armory with all of the other guns. When Red Herring points out that one of the first things Jeff said to them was that he didn't intend to give it away, quipping "Bro, try finding some Adderall under an authoritarian regime and then we can talk about that." in response to further pushback from Red Herring. Cutting their conversation short, Jeff resumes shooting.

Author Comments

  • The title of the chapter is a reference to a post on Bluesky and a play on words referencing the fact that Jeff is literally trying to kill Coach as Las Vegas floods from the heavy rainfall. This title receives an answer in the form of chapter 28's title.
  • This is the first chapter to feature multimedia.[citation needed] It features a dub of Jigsaw's monologue and an image for an author's note at the end.
  • Although it's not something I considered while writing, Luigi's attempt to diffuse the situation could also be because he's also benefitted from nepotism and so Kenny and Coach's words made him uncomfortable.
