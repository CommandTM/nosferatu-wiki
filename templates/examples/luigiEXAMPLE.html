Luigi Mangione

"the Red Savior marks the beginning of the end."
- Luigi Mangione, Rumination

Luigi Mangione is a member of The Resistance. His family is said to be very important, being one of the founding families of The Resistance. While in real life he's best known for being the main suspect in the assassination of United Healthcare CEO Brian Thompson, the fanfic states that he never killed Brian, and simply liked the attention.
